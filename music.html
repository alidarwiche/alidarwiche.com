Music Production

I've been looking forward to starting this journey for a long time. The first major I ever seriously considered was Audio Engineering, and I've never forgotten that gut instinct. If you haven't read my section on Voice, you might not know that I've been training as a singer for the past six years. What I haven't mentioned is that I have also been writing music for the past four years. On my phone I have about 400 voice memos of song ideas and no idea how to take them from my head to yours. Thus, I've decided to pursue an Associate's degree in Music Production from Austin Community College. The music production program at ACC has fantastic resources, great instructors, evening classes, and all at an affordable price. As I work to grow as a music producer, I will also continue my growth as a musician by learning piano, guitar, violin, and even more about the voice. It's a lot to do, but I'm in no rush. :)

If you want to be in the know for when I start sharing music online, follow me on Twitter or YouTube.
